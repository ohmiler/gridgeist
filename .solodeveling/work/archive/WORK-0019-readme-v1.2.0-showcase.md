## v1.2.0 system contract

- **Color:** Keep paper, ink, Northline blue, acid focus, orange exception, and green
  online roles; expose them through foundation and semantic tokens with explicit
  foreground/background and state relationships.
- **Typography:** Keep Inter for display/body and DM Mono for operational metadata;
  consolidate display, heading, body, label, and metadata roles.
- **Layout and spacing:** Use one outer container, a connected split hero, a dominant
  topology stage, and a shared spacing rhythm that recomposes by priority on mobile.
- **Surface and shape:** Use square operational surfaces and rules for adjacency;
  avoid decorative radius, shadow, and gradient treatments in the After view.
- **Components and states:** Give navigation, action, status, route-row, and metric
  families shared anatomy plus applicable default, hover, focus, active/current,
  online, and attention behavior without relying on color alone.
- **Motion:** Animate only route causality and entry hierarchy, with reduced-motion
  fallback.
